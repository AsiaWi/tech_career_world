Could you tell me about your current career: what is it you do now? What does a typical day look like for you? How does your work involve technology, digital and data?

I am a data test engineer. A typical day for me involves looking at data we receive about the performance of schools in primary, secondary and 16 to 18 tests. We get the data in an excel spreadsheet. I then review it to check that the values given are what we expect them to be. If they are then I work with the developer on our team to get the data put in a database called cosmos. I use Structured Query Language (SQL) to query the data. This database is then used to populate the data that you can see on compare school and college performance website. When I am not checking data, I am working with the rest of the team to understand changes we need to make to the site. This involves working with a business analyst and the people who drive the change to agree what should happen in our work.

I didn't start out thinking I would end up in a career in technology. I studied english and history at university. I got really involved in this and this led to me becoming a test analyst! My enthusiasm for problem solving, analysis and wanting to provide a great service is the main driver for how I got into a career in technology and why I stay. The positive impact you can make in people's lives through tech is a great part of the role I do.
